Ghana Education Service Recruitment Portal.

The Ghana Education Service is responsible for recruiting and posting trained and qualified
teachers and non-teaching staff to public basic and second-cycle schools in Ghana. The
recruitment is typically done, depending on government approval and available vacancies.

The GhanaTeachers Recruitment Process is official procedure through which
trained and qualified teachers are employed into public schools by the
Ghana Education Service (GES).

GRADUATE
APPLICANTS

The category is for graduates who've successfully completed their various programs from various accredited tertiary institutions.

Phase 01

Eligibility Requirements

To apply for Recruitment, an applicant must fulfill the following initial requirements to initiate the evaluation process:

Academic Qualification:
  • Hold a Diploma in Basic Education (DBE), Honours In Diploma (HND), Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), Masters Degree or any recognized education-related degree.
  • Have completed training from an accredited institution such as:
    • University of Education, Winneba
    • University of Cape Coast
    • Any accredited College of Education in Ghana.
National Service:
  • Completed National Service.
  • Possess a valid National Service Certificate.
Licensure Examination:
  • Passed the Ghana Teacher Licensure Examination conducted by the National Teaching Council (NTC).
  • Have a valid teaching license number.
Other Requirements:
  • Ghana Card (National ID)
  • SSNIT number
  • Passport-size photographs

Phase 02

Online Application Process

Applications are submitted online through the official GES-GEDP recruitment portal. Please follow this step-by-step procedure:

Step 1: Access GEDP Recruitment Portal
  • Use the link provided or log on the gedp.gov.gh search for Recruitment Portal from External Portal.
  • Navigate to Teaching Staff tab.
  • Click on Apply Here.
Step 2: Fill Out the Application Form

Provide accurate information including:

  • Personal details
  • Academic qualifications
  • NTC license number
  • National Service details
  • Preferred region/district
Step 3: Upload Documents
  • Certificates
  • Licensure certificate
  • National Service cert.
  • Ghana Card
Step 4: Finalize
  • Review details carefully.
  • Submit the form.
  • Print confirmation slip.

Phase 03

Shortlisting and Validation

After successfully submitting your application, the evaluation process begins:

  • GES screens applications to ensure all requirements are met.
  • GES approves and releases the list of Shortlisted applicants.

Phase 04

Posting and Appointment

Successful applicants will proceed to the final stage:

  • Receive official appointment letters from the Ghana Education Service.
  • Are posted to schools based on:
    • • Subject specialization
    • • Regional preference
    • • Vacancy availability

Applicants must report to the assigned district office to complete documentation before assuming duty.

Important Notes
for Applicants:

  • Recruitment is merit-based and transparent.
  • Applicants should avoid middlemen or unauthorized agents.
  • Always verify information from official GES communication channels.
  • Posting may be outside your preferred region depending on need.
  • False information or forged documents leads to disqualification.
  • Posting to deprived areas may be prioritized.

NON-TEACHING
STAFF

Non-teaching staff in the Ghana Education Service (GES) are employees who support the administration and smooth running of public education offices but are not directly involved in classroom teaching.

They play essential roles in ensuring that teaching and learning take place effectively by handling administrative, financial, technical, and support services.

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS FOR NON-TEACHING STAFF

1️⃣ Educational Qualification

For Administrative/Professional Roles
  • Bachelor’s degree or Higher National Diploma (HND) in relevant fields such as:
    • Administration
    • Accounting
    • HR Management
    • ICT
    • Procurement
    • Statistics
    • Social Sciences
  • From accredited institutions such as:
    • University of Ghana
    • KNUST
    • University of Cape Coast
For Clerical or Technical Roles
  • HND, Diploma, or Technical Certificate in a relevant field.
  • WASSCE certificate (for junior/support staff positions).

2️⃣ Professional Certification

(Where Applicable)

Some roles may require:

  • Membership with a professional body (e.g., accounting or procurement body).
  • Specialized training certificates.
  • ICT certifications (for IT roles).

3️⃣ National Service

  • Applicants must have completed National Service (for graduate positions).
  • Must provide a valid National Service Certificate.

Online Application Process for Non-Teaching Staff

Applications are submitted online through the official GES-GEDP recruitment portal.

Step-by-Step Process:
Step 1: Access GEDP Recruitment Portal.
  • Use the link provided or log on the gedp.gov.gh search for Recruitment Portal from External Portal.
  • Navigate to Non-Teaching Staff tab.
  • Click on Apply Here.
Step 2: Fill Out the Application Form

Provide accurate information including:

  • Personal details
  • Academic qualifications
  • NTC license number
  • National Service details
  • Preferred region/district
Step 3: Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of:

  • Certificates
  • Licensure certificate
  • National Service certificate
  • Ghana Card
Step 4: Submit and Print Confirmation
  • Review all details carefully.
  • Submit the form.
  • Print the confirmation slip for reference.

3️⃣ Screening and Shortlisting

GES reviews applications to ensure:

  • Required qualifications are met.
  • Documents are authentic.
  • Experience matches job requirements.

Shortlisted candidates are invited for interview.

4️⃣ Interview

The interview panel assesses:

  • Knowledge
  • Communication skills
  • Work experience
  • Professional competence
  • Integrity

⚠️ Applicants must bring original documents for verification.

5️⃣ Appointment and Posting

Successful candidates:

  • Receive appointment letters.
  • Are assigned to schools, district offices, or regional offices.
  • Complete documentation for payroll processing.

6️⃣ Assumption of Duty

After completing all documentation:

  • The staff member officially begins work.

Important Notes

  • Recruitment is merit-based and transparent.
  • Applicants should avoid middlemen or unauthorized agents.
  • Always verify information from official GES communication channels.
  • Posting may be outside your preferred region depending on need.
  • False information or forged documents leads to disqualification.
  • Posting to deprived areas may be prioritized.

College Of Education
Applicants.

This recruitment process is also specifically for trained teachers who graduate from accredited Colleges of Education in Ghana and are seeking employment in public basic schools.

The Ghana Education Service recruits newly trained qualified teachers from Colleges of Education into public basic schools (Kindergarten, Primary, and Junior High School) based on government approval and availability of vacancies.

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

To qualify for recruitment, a College of Education graduate must meet the following conditions:

1 Academic Qualification

  • Must have completed a Diploma in Basic Education (DBE) or Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) from an accredited College of Education.
  • Must have successfully completed all academic and teaching practice requirements.

2 Teacher Licensure

  • Must have passed the Ghana Teacher Licensure Examination conducted by the National Teaching Council (NTC).
  • Must possess a valid NTC license number.

3 National Service

  • Must have completed National Service.
  • Must possess a National Service Certificate.

Required Documents

Applicants must have:

  • Academic certificate
  • Licensure certificate
  • National Service certificate
  • Ghana Card
  • SSNIT number
  • Passport-size photograph

Online Application Process

Applications are submitted online through the official GES-GEDP recruitment portal.

Step-by-Step Process:
Step 1: Access GEDP Recruitment Portal
  • Use the link provided or log on the gedp.gov.gh search for Recruitment Portal from External Portal.
  • Navigate to Teaching Staff tab.
  • Click on Apply Here.
Step 2: Fill Out the Application Form

Provide accurate information including:

  • Personal details
  • Academic qualifications
  • NTC license number
  • National Service details
  • Preferred region/district
Step 3: Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of:

  • Certificates
  • Licensure certificate
  • National Service certificate
  • Ghana Card
Step 4: Submit and Print Confirmation
  • Review all details carefully.
  • Submit the form.
  • Print the confirmation slip for reference.

Shortlisting and Validation

After submission:

  • GES screens applications.
  • GES approves Shortlisted applicants.

Posting and Appointment

Successful applicants:

  • Receive official appointment letters.
  • Are posted to schools based on:
    • Subject specialization
    • Regional preference
    • Vacancy availability

Applicants must report to the assigned district office to complete documentation before assuming duty.

Original documents must be presented for verification.

7 Assumption of Duty

After receiving posting:

  • Applicant reports to the District Education Office.
  • Completes documentation.
  • Receives formal introduction letter to the school.
  • Begins teaching duties.

Important Notes & Conclusion

  • Recruitment is merit-based and transparent.
  • Applicants should avoid middlemen or unauthorized agents.
  • Always verify information from official GES communication channels.
  • Posting may be outside your preferred region depending on need.
  • False information or forged documents leads to disqualification.
  • Posting to deprived areas may be prioritized.
The GES Recruitment Exercise for College of Education graduates is a structured process designed to ensure that qualified, licensed, and professionally trained teachers are employed into Ghana’s public basic schools. Meeting all eligibility requirements and carefully following the application procedure increases the chances of successful recruitment.
